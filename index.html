Famous Electrical Engineers.

Thomas Edison - holds an astounding 1,097 patents. He was an American inventor and businessman.
Gustav Kirchhoff - physicist who contributed to electrical circuits, spectroscopy and black-body radiation.
James Clerk Maxwell - was mathematician and scientist responsible for the classical theory of electromagnetic radiation.
Nikola Tesla - was a investor, electrical engineer, mechanical engineer and futurist.
